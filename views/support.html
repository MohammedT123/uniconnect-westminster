Student Support Services

The University of Westminster offers a wide range of free support services. Find the right help for you below — all contact details are listed directly on this page.

🚨

Need urgent help?

If you or someone else is in immediate danger call 999. For urgent mental health support call the Samaritans on 116 123 (free, 24/7). The Student Wellbeing team is available during office hours on 020 7911 5000 ext 66899.

Disability Learning Support
Support for students with disabilities including dyslexia, dyspraxia, AD(H)D, mental health conditions, hearing impairment, visual impairment, mobility issues and long term health conditions. The Equality Act defines disability as a physical or mental impairment which has a substantial and long-term adverse effect on a person's ability to perform normal day-to-day activities.
Webwestminster.ac.uk/disability
EmailDLS@westminster.ac.uk
Phone020 7911 5000 ext 68800
NoteOr speak to your faculty Disability Tutor — see website for contact details
