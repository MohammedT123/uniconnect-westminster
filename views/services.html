📚 Academic Services
Manage your studies, results and academic records
🎓
Student Hub
Your central hub for all university services. Access your student record, timetable, results and all key student systems from one place.
Open Student Hub ↗ 📊
Exam Results (My Student Record)
View your module results, assessment grades and progression decisions. Log in with your Westminster username and password to access your record.
View results ↗ 📋
Module Registration
Register for your modules each academic year. Complete module registration before the deadline to avoid any disruption to your studies.
Register modules ↗ 🗓️
Timetable
View your personal lecture and seminar timetable. Check room allocations, lecture times and any timetable changes announced by your department.
View timetable ↗ 💻
Blackboard (VLE)
Access your course materials, lecture slides, assignment briefs and submission portals. Your primary learning platform for all modules at Westminster.
Open Blackboard ↗ 📝
Exams and Assessments
Find information about exam schedules, assessment guidelines, extenuating circumstances and the university academic regulations and appeals process.
View exams info ↗
💰 Finance and Administration
Manage your fees, funding and official documents
💳
Fees and Funding
View your tuition fee account, check payment deadlines, set up payment plans and find information about bursaries, scholarships and emergency funding.
Manage fees ↗ 🏦
Student Finance England
Manage your student loan, check payment dates and update your details with Student Finance England. Loan payments are made three times per year.
Student Finance ↗ 📄
Official Documents
Request official letters, enrollment certificates, council tax exemption letters and transcripts. Usually processed within five working days of request.
Request documents ↗
🤝 Support Services
Get help when you need it
🧠
Student Wellbeing
Access free counselling, mental health support and disability services. You can self-refer online and appointments are available in person and remotely.
Get support ↗ 💼
Careers and Employability
Book appointments with careers advisors, get CV feedback, find graduate jobs and internships and access Westminster employer network and events.
Visit careers ↗ 🖥️
IT Support
Get help with your Westminster login, email, software access and campus computers. IT support is available via phone, email and walk-in at the IT helpdesk.
IT helpdesk ↗ 📖
Library Services
Access physical and digital library resources, book study spaces, request books and get support from subject librarians for your assignments and research.
Library portal ↗ 🏛️
Students Union (UWSU)
Your student union — societies, events, student representation, advice and advocacy. The SU is here to support you and represent your interests to the university.
Visit UWSU ↗ 🌍
International Student Support
Specialist support for international students including visa guidance, BRP collection, UKVI compliance and support with settling into life in the UK.
International support ↗
